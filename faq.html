Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

General Questions

How do I sign up for the app?

Download the app from your device's app store, then open it and follow the on-screen instructions to create an account. You'll need to provide some basic information like your name, email address, and phone number.

Which areas do you deliver to?

Our service is available in most major cities and towns. You can check the availability in your area by entering your address in the app. If we're not yet in your area, stay tunedâ€”we're expanding rapidly!

Ordering

Can I customize my order?

Absolutely! Most menu items have customization options for ingredients, sizes, and add-ons. Just select your preferences before adding the item to your cart.

Is there a minimum order amount?

No! Order as much as your heart desires.

Delivery

How long will my delivery take?

Delivery times vary based on the restaurant's preparation time, order volume, and your distance from the restaurant. After placing an order, you'll receive an estimated delivery time. You can also track the delivery in real-time in the app.

Can I schedule a delivery for a later time?

Yes, you can! During checkout, choose the "Schedule" option and select a delivery date and time that works best for you.
